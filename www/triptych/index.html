Triptych Proposals

Three HTML Proposals to increase its expressive power as hypermedia:
  1. Forms should be able to make PUT, PATCH, and DELETE requests
  2. Buttons should be able to issue HTTP requests, independent of forms
  3. Links, Buttons, and Forms should be able to target and replace a DOM subtree
I first outlined these proposals in The Life & Death of htmx at Big Sky Dev Con.
