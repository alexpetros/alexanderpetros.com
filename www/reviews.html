SFT

As with the Fountain, the SFT’s existence depends on how the people engage(d) with it. The artists chose to destroy the contract so that no one could purchase the piece; in some ways, it is a large-scale public artwork for the internet age. In making the work Kolman and Petros sought to make an artwork that trolls on the expectations of uniqueness and ownership that are associated with NFT art. If anyone can alter it, what is the work?
